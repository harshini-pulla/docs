## **Create Recruitment Campaign**

Click **New Campaign**

## **A. General Info**

Fill in the required details:

- **Select Profile Articulation**
- **Campaign Name**
- **Status** (Planning / Active / On Hold / Completed)
- **Company Name**
- **Company Email**
- **Start Date**
- **End Date**
- **Salary Offered Range** (Min / Max)
- **Vendor Rate**

Click **Save** to create the base campaign.

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 140433.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28140433.png)

## **B. Pre-Screening Form**

### **Purpose**

The Pre-Screening Form is the **first evaluation test** sent to candidates after they are shortlisted.\
It helps recruiters verify fundamental eligibility before interviews.

### **Steps**

- Enter **Form Title** (default: "Pre-screening Form")
- Add questions using **Add Question**
- For each question:
  - Enter **Question Text**
  - Select **Input Type** (Text, Number, Yes/No, Select)
  - Mark **Required** if needed
- Click **Preview Form** to see how the candidate will view it
- Click **Save**

### **How it works in the system**

- After a candidate is marked **Fit**, the system sends this pre-screening form link via email.
- Candidate responses appear in:\
  **Campaign → Candidate → Pre-Screening Responses**

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 140510.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28140510.png)

## **C. Question Bank**

### **Purpose**

The Question Bank is used by the **AI interview engine** to ask relevant questions and evaluate candidate answers.

### **Steps**

- Click **Add Question**
- Enter **Question**
- Enter the **Expected Answer**
- Add multiple Q&A pairs as needed
- Click **Save Questions**

### **How it works in the system**

- The AI uses these questions during interviews to:
  - Evaluate candidate responses
  - Generate interview summaries
  - Produce fit/no-fit recommendations
  - Maintain consistent assessment across candidates

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 140545.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28140545.png)

## **View Campaign**

- Click the **eye icon** on any campaign.
- View:
  - Complete role details
  - Salary, budget & status
  - Linked Profile Articulation
  - Pre-screening form
  - Question bank
  - Assigned recruiters

Used for reviewing information before editing.

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 140612.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28140612.png)

## **Edit Campaign** 

- Click **Edit** on the selected campaign.
- Update:
  - Status
  - Timeline
  - Salary or Budget
  - Profile Articulation
  - Company details
- Click **Save**\
  → Changes reflect instantly.

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 140643.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28140643.png)

## **Delete Campaign**

- Click **Delete**
- Confirm deletion
- Campaign is removed permanently (if not linked to critical dependencies).

![image.png](/images/image.png)

## **Search & Filter Campaigns**

You can filter campaigns by:

- Status (Active, Planning, Completed)
- Client name
- Profile Articulation
- Campaign title

Search supports keywords in:

- Campaign name
- Company name
- Profile name

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 140849.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28140849.png)

## **Where Is Recruitment Campaign Used?**

Recruitment Campaigns link directly to:

- **Candidate Tracking**
- **Interview Scheduling**
- **Pre-Screening Responses**
- **AI Interview Evaluations**
- **Client Notes & Approvals**
- **CV Feedback & Representation Emails**

All downstream workflows rely on the **campaignId**.

## **Summary**

Recruitment Campaign acts as the **core hiring container**.\
Every candidate action — shortlisting, screening, interviewing, feedback, evaluation — is tied to a specific campaign.\
It ensures structured execution of hiring processes across clients and roles.
