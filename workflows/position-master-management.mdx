# **Position Master Workflow**

Manage standardized job positions and their associated industries across the RMS.

### **Add Job Position**

- Click **New Job Position**
- Enter:
  - Position Name
  - Industry
  - Description (optional)
- Save\
  New position appears in the list immediately.

![Screenshot 2025-11-27 170912.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-27170912.png)

### **Edit Job Position**

- Click **Edit** on any row
- Update name, industry, or description
- Save changes\
  Updated information reflects everywhere Position Master is used.

![Screenshot 2025-11-27 170935.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-27170935.png)

### **View Job Position**

- Click **View** (eye icon)
- View full details: title, industry, description, timestamps.

![Screenshot 2025-11-27 170925.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-27170925.png)

### **Delete Job Position**

- Click **Delete**
- Confirm deletion
- Position is removed if allowed by business rules.

![Screenshot 2025-11-27 170945.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-27170945.png)

### **Search**

Search supports:

- **Position name**
- **Industry**

![Screenshot 2025-11-27 171337.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-27171337.png)

## **Where Position Master is Used**

### **Target Company Master**

- Used to assign **target positions** for each company
- Helps BD & recruitment teams focus on specific roles
- Position list comes directly from Position Master API

# **summary**

**Position Master is the authoritative catalog of job positions used across the RMS, ensuring consistency and powering features like Target Company role assignments.**
