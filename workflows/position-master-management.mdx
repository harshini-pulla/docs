### **Add Job Position**

- Click **New Job Position**
- Enter:
  - Position Name
  - Industry
  - Description (optional)
- Save\
  New position appears in the list immediately.

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 121331.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28121331.png)

### **Edit Job Position**

- Click **Edit** on any row
- Update name, industry, or description
- Save changes\
  Updated information reflects everywhere Position Master is used.

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 121355.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28121355.png)

### **View Job Position**

- Click **View** (eye icon)
- View full details: title, industry, description, timestamps.

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 121343.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28121343.png)

### **Delete Job Position**

- Click **Delete**
- Confirm deletion
- Position is removed if allowed by business rules.

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 121406.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28121406.png)

### **Search**

Search supports:

- **Position name**
- **Industry**

![Screenshot 2025-11-28 121431.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-28121431.png)

## **Where Position Master is Used**

### **Target Company Master**

- Used to assign **target positions** for each company
- Helps BD & recruitment teams focus on specific roles
- Position list comes directly from Position Master API

## **Summary**

**Position Master is the authoritative catalog of job positions used across the RMS, ensuring consistency and powering features like Target Company role assignments.**
