## Add Client

- Click **Add Client**
- Fill required fields:
  - Company Name
  - Industry
  - Relationship Type
  - Contact person, email, phone
  - Location
- Set **Status** (Active / Inactive)
- Save the client

New client appears in the list immediately.

![Screenshot 2025-12-01 111846.png](/images/Screenshot2025-12-01111846.png)

## Edit Client

- Click **Edit** on any client card
- Update company name, industry, contact details, or relationship type
- Save changes

Updated info syncs with JD, Campaign, and Profile screens.

![Screenshot 2025-12-01 111911.png](/images/Screenshot2025-12-01111911.png)

## View Client

- Click **View** (eye icon)
- View full details: industries, contacts, website, description, activity logs

Used for quick review before updating.

![Screenshot 2025-12-01 111859.png](/images/Screenshot2025-12-01111859.png)

## Delete Client

- Click **Delete**
- Confirm deletion
- Client is removed if not linked to critical active items

![Screenshot 2025-12-01 111926.png](/images/Screenshot2025-12-01111926.png)

## Search & Filter

You can filter by:

- Status (Active / Inactive)
- Relationship Type (Direct, Direct Recruitment)
- Industry (IT, Oil & Gas, etc.)

Search supports:

- Company name
- Contact name
- Email

![Screenshot 2025-12-01 112124.png](/images/Screenshot2025-12-01112124.png)

## How It Fits With Other Modules

### **JD Creation**

- Company dropdown is populated from Client Master
- Selecting a client **auto-fills the company email**

### **Campaign Creation**

- Campaign inherits company name/email from Client Master if missing

## Summary

Client Master is the **central directory of all client companies**.  
All JDs, Campaigns, Positions, and Profiles depend on this module to ensure consistent and accurate company data across the RMS.
