# **Target Company Master Workflow**

This workflow describes how admins manage prospective or strategic companies inside the RMS.

## **Add Target Company**

- Click **Add Company**
- Fill required fields:
  - Company Name
  - Industry
  - Category
  - Company Size
  - Priority (High / Medium / Low)
  - Contact person, email, phone
  - Location
  - Status (Active / Inactive)
- Add optional details:
  - Key contacts
  - Target positions
  - Website
  - Notes
- Click **Save**
  The new company appears in the list immediately.

## **Edit Target Company**

- Click **Edit** on any company row
- Update:
  - Industry
  - Priority
  - Status
  - Contacts
  - Location
  - Business details
  - Key contacts
  - Target positions
- Save changes\
  Updated data instantly reflects in the Target Company table.


## **View Target Company**

- Click **View** (eye icon)
- View complete details:
  - Company information
  - Contact details
  - Key contacts
  - Target positions
  - Website, notes, description
  - Last updated date

## **Delete Target Company**

- Click **Delete**
- Confirm deletion
- Company is removed **if not linked to any active dependency**

## **Search & Filter**

You can filter companies by:

- Priority (High / Medium / Low)
- Status (Active / Inactive)
- Industry (IT, Oil & Gas, Healthcare, etc.)
- Category

Search supports:

- Company name
- Location
- Industry
- Category

## **How It Fits With Other Modules**

### **Category Management**

- Sub-Sub Categories use this module to populate the **Select a Company** dropdown.
- Ensures every specialization links to the right company targets.
