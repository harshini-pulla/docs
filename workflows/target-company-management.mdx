# **Target Company Master Workflow**

This workflow describes how admins manage prospective or strategic companies inside the RMS.

## **Add Target Company**

- Click **Add Company**
- Fill required fields:
  - Company Name
  - Industry
  - Category
  - Company Size
  - Priority (High / Medium / Low)
  - Contact person, email, phone
  - Location
  - Status (Active / Inactive)
- Add optional details:
  - Key contacts
  - Target positions
  - Website
  - Notes
- Save the company\
  New target company appears in the list immediately.

## **Edit Target Company**

- Click **Edit** on any company row
- Update industry, priority, status, contacts, location, or business details
- Add or modify key contacts and target positions
- Save changes\
  Updated data instantly reflects in the Target Company table.

## **View Target Company**

- Click **View** (eye icon)
- View full details:
  - Company information
  - Contact details
  - Key contacts
  - Target positions
  - Notes, website, description
  - Last updated date

Used for review before editing.

## **Delete Target Company**

- Click **Delete**
- Confirm deletion
- Company is removed if it is not tied to any active dependency\
  (soft restrictions depend on your business rules)

## **Search & Filter**

You can filter by:

- **Priority** (High / Medium / Low)
- **Status** (Active / Inactive)
- **Industry** (IT, Oil & Gas, Healthcare, etc.)
- **Category**

Search supports:

- **Company name**
- **Location**
- **Industry**
- **Category**

## **How It Fits With Other Modules**

### **Category Management**

- Category Management uses the **Target Company Master** to populate company lists inside **Sub-Sub Categories**.
- When adding or editing a sub-sub category, the “Select a Company” dropdown shows companies directly fetched from Target Company Master.
- This links each specialization or role category with the relevant companies you are targeting.

## **Summary**

Target Company Master helps your team track and prioritize companies you want to pursue for future recruitment business.\
It stores industry, size, contacts, priority, and target roles, enabling BD and recruitment teams to stay aligned.\
This module acts as a structured pipeline of strategic prospects for long-term business development.
